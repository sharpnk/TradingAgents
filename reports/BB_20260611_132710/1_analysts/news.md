- The stock has priced in a tremendous amount of optimism (+180% in 90 days).
- Profit-taking has already begun (-4.84% in the most recent session).
- The debate between "meme mania" and "growth story" remains unresolved.

**The bull case** rests on QNX becoming the dominant OS for physical AI (robotics, autonomous vehicles, industrial automation), which could justify a higher multiple. **The bear case** rests on macro headwinds and the risk that the rally has overshot fundamentals.
