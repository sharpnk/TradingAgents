## 1. 🚀 BREAKING — Salesforce Acquires Fin for $3.6 Billion (The Defining Event)

The dominant news story this week is Salesforce's **acquisition of Fin** (formerly Intercom), an AI-powered customer service platform, for **$3.6 billion**.

### Key Details:
- **What Fin does:** Develops AI agents that handle customer queries autonomously across chat, email, WhatsApp, SMS, phone, and Slack. Fin's AI reportedly **resolves 76% of support queries without human intervention**.
- **Strategic Rationale:** The acquisition directly bolsters **Agentforce**, Salesforce's existing enterprise AI agent platform. Agentforce's annual recurring revenue has already **more than tripled to $1.2 billion in Q1 2026**.
- **Integration Plan:** Salesforce will use Fin's team and technology to enhance Agentforce's capabilities.
