The Neutral's trim-to-underweight approach is the only strategy that respects all conflicting signals without requiring a prediction of the binary outcome.

The Aggressive Analyst's full-sell argument is not without merit—momentum is clearly fading—but it overweights short-term technical signals against a still-intact intermediate uptrend and a genuine fundamental catalyst. The Conservative Analyst's full-hold approach underweights the very real gap-down risk and the fact that a 44% rally already prices in significant optimism. Reducing to underweight locks in a meaningful portion of the rally while keeping skin in the game. The stop at $14.50 (below the 50/200 SMA cluster and the Bollinger mid-band) provides a disciplined exit if the uptrend structurally breaks.
