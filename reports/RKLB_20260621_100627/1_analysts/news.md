## 1. RKLB-SPECIFIC CATALYSTS & DEVELOPMENTS

### 🔥 CRITICAL CATALYST: Nasdaq-100 Inclusion (Effective June 22, 2026)
The single most impactful near-term catalyst for RKLB. This milestone triggers **mandatory share purchases** from passive funds, ETFs, and index trackers that follow the benchmark. However, SpaceX shares have since **fallen 6%+** as the post-IPO frenzy fades (Reuters). Analysts and articles now suggest capital is rotating **back into RKLB** as a more reasonably valued pure-play space stock. RKLB +308% over the past year vs. the speculative frenzy around SpaceX's ~$2.5T valuation.
