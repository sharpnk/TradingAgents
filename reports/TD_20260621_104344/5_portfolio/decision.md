The sentiment reading of 7.5/10 bullish, driven by genuine catalysts rather than speculative hype, is a double-edged sword: it reflects institutional accumulation (healthy) but also suggests limited marginal buyers remain (a contrarian warning). Reddit silence is a net positive—no retail froth—but the absence of bearish voices at an all-time-high RSI is itself a cautionary signal.

Hold is the appropriate rating. The fundamentals prevent a Sell or Underweight; the technical overextension and near-term catalyst gap prevent a Buy or Overweight. The disciplined path is to maintain positions, enforce risk management through a stop, and wait for either a pullback that resets risk/reward or a fresh catalyst (Q3 earnings beat, accelerated buybacks, analyst upgrades) that justifies upgrading the rating.
