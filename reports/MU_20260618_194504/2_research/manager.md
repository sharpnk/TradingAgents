The bull's long-term structural thesis carries the argument—the AI memory super-cycle is real, multi-year, and MU is a primary beneficiary with pricing power that the market has not fully re-rated. A Hold would underweight a genuinely transformative fundamental story. Overweight captures the constructive view: the direction is up over the investment horizon, but position-building should be gradual and tactical, using pullbacks to the identified support zones rather than chasing the current price.
