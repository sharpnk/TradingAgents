## 11. ACTIONABLE INSIGHTS FOR TRADERS

1. **VALUE TRAP WARNING:** The 9.74% dividend yield and low P/B (1.70x) may appear cheap, but the extreme leverage (190% D/E), negative tangible equity, and recent quarterly weakness suggest caution.

2. **KEY CATALYST TO MONITOR:** FCF trajectory. After surging 61% in 2025 to $2.35B, Q1 2026 posted negative FCF (-$25M). If CapEx normalizes back down to ~$600-650M/quarter, FCF should recover. If elevated CapEx persists, the dividend could be at risk.

3. **DEBT REFINANCING RISK:** With $31.5B in debt and interest expense at $1.5B (2025), rising rates would pressure earnings. The interest coverage ratio (EBIT/Interest) was ~1.8x in 2025 — tight, but manageable.

4. **DIVIDEND SAFETY:** Full-year 2025 FCF covered dividends 1.44x. However, with net income of only $1.1B vs dividends of $1.63B, the dividend is not covered by earnings — it's funded by depreciation and amortization (non-cash charges). This is common for telecoms but still a risk.

5. **SEASONAL PATTERN:** Q1 typically has higher CapEx (network build season). If Q2-Q4 2026 CapEx moderates, FCF should recover.

6. **FORWARD P/E OPPORTUNITY:** At 17x forward earnings (vs 28x trailing), the market expects significant EPS recovery to ~$0.99/share. If realized, this supports the current price.
