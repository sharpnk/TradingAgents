## 1. COMPANY OVERVIEW & BUSINESS MODEL

AST SpaceMobile is building the first global space-based cellular broadband network, designed to provide direct-to-cell connectivity (4G/5G) from low-earth orbit (LEO) satellites to standard smartphones without requiring any modifications or additional hardware. The company partners with major mobile network operators (MNOs) worldwide.
