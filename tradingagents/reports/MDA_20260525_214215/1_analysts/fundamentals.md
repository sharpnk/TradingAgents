## 7. Growth Drivers & Risks

### Growth Drivers:
1. **Massive revenue growth:** 51% YoY revenue increase from FY 2023 to FY 2024.
2. **Strong backlog:** Deferred revenue of $710.7M indicates a healthy pipeline of future work.
3. **Secular tailwinds:** Global space economy, defense modernization, satellite internet/direct-to-device, and government space programs.
4. **Infrastructure investment:** Heavy capex ($275M in FY 2024) on new facilities to support future capacity.
5. **Balance sheet strengthening:** Cash infusion from equity raise provides financial flexibility.

### Key Risks:
1. **High valuation:** P/E of 74x leaves no room for earnings disappointment.
2. **Margin pressure:** Gross margins declining from 35.6% to ~25% as revenue scales.
3. **Flat net income:** Despite revenue surging, net income has stayed in the $24-33M/quarter range.
4. **Dilution:** ~10% share count increase from equity raise dilutes existing shareholders.
5. **Heavy intangibles:** $1.68B in goodwill/intangibles — if growth disappoints, impairment risk exists.
6. **Negative FCF in recent quarter:** Heavy capex is consuming operating cash flow.

1. **Momentum is strong but valuation is stretched.** The stock's 52-week range ($23.23–$43.66) and proximity to the high end suggest strong momentum, but at 74x earnings, any earnings miss could trigger a sharp correction.

2. **Watch for margin inflection.** If gross margins stabilize or improve, net income could expand dramatically given the revenue base ($2.1B). Q1 2026 gross margin was ~24.8%, roughly flat sequentially.

3. **The equity raise was a double-edged sword.** It strengthened the balance sheet but diluted existing holders. The ~$442M infusion at/near the 52-week high suggests management capitalized on a favorable stock price — prudent but dilutive.

4. **Key catalysts to monitor:**
   - Next quarterly earnings (margin trajectory)
   - Major contract wins (backlog growth)
   - Capex completion (new facilities coming online)
   - Debt refinancing or further capital markets activity

5. **Risk/Reward is asymmetric.** The high multiple means downside risk is significant on any disappointment, while the growth story (if executed) supports the forward P/E of ~36x.
