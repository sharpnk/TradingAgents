## FINAL ASSESSMENT

**Dell Technologies** is in a strong growth phase driven by AI infrastructure demand. The company posted record revenue of $113.5B in FY2025 with $8.55B in free cash flow. The Forward P/E of 18.38x is reasonable given the expected EPS growth to $17.25 (from $8.68). However, the balance sheet has structural weaknesses (negative equity, high debt, current ratio < 1.0) that bear watching.

The stock has already rallied significantly (52-wk high $327.73 vs low $106.38). At ~$320, much of the AI optimism appears priced in. The key question is whether Dell can continue to execute and deliver on the elevated forward EPS expectations.

**Bottom Line:** Dell is a high-quality beneficiary of the AI infrastructure buildout with strong cash flows and aggressive capital returns. The risk/reward is balanced with limited upside to the 52-week high (~2-3%) but significant downside if growth disappoints.
