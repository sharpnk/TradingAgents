## 1. Company Overview

Algonquin Power & Utilities Corp. is a diversified utility holding company with operations across North America. The company has been undergoing a major strategic transformation — exiting its renewable energy business (sold in 2024) to focus exclusively on **regulated utility operations** (electric, gas, water, and wastewater). This restructuring resulted in significant impairments in 2024 but set the stage for a more stable, utility-focused business model going forward.

## 7. Strategic Developments & Outlook

1. **Business Transformation Complete:** The sale of the renewable energy business (wind, solar, hydro) in 2024 removed a volatile, capital-intensive segment. AQN is now a **pure-play regulated utility** with more predictable earnings.
2. **Deleveraging Priority:** Management has signaled debt reduction is a priority. The D/E ratio has improved from 149% (2023) to 133% (2026).
3. **Dividend Sustainability Risk:** The 4.31% dividend yield is tempting, but with persistent negative FCF, there is a risk of a dividend cut if debt reduction takes precedence.
4. **Rate Base Growth:** As a regulated utility, AQN benefits from rate base growth and inflation-indexed rate increases.
