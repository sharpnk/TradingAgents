## 🏢 Company Overview

Enbridge Inc. is a Canadian multinational energy transportation company and one of the world's largest midstream energy infrastructure companies. It operates the world's longest crude oil and liquids transportation system, with extensive natural gas pipelines, storage, and gas distribution utilities across North America.

**Key Insight:** The dividend is well-covered by operating cash flow (70% payout ratio), but **NOT covered by free cash flow** (278% payout ratio in 2025). This means Enbridge is borrowing or selling assets to fund dividends beyond what FCF supports. The 4.99% yield is attractive, but sustainability relies on capex eventually generating incremental cash flow.
