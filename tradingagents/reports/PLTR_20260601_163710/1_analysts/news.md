---

### 🚀 1. Nvidia Computex Catalyst – Broad AI Software Rally (June 1)

The most significant event for PLTR was **Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang's keynote at Computex**, where he explicitly dismissed the "SaaSpocalypse" narrative — the fear that AI agents would render traditional software companies obsolete. Huang stated: *"There are going to be so many agents... software companies are not going out of business."*

**Impact:** Software stocks rallied broadly on June 1. Palantir rose alongside peers like ServiceNow (NOW), Snowflake, and Dell. The WSJ, IBD, and Investing.com all reported on this sector-wide surge. PLTR was noted as "eyeing an early buy point" by Investor's Business Daily.

---

### 💻 2. Dell Earnings Validates AI Infrastructure Build-Out

Dell and Palantir have an established partnership, and Dell's strong AI hardware demand confirms that the downstream software layer (where PLTR operates) will benefit from the AI infrastructure cycle.
