## 1. Company Overview

FirstCash Holdings, Inc. is a leading operator of retail pawn stores and provider of consumer financial services across the U.S. and Latin America. The company operates under the **FirstCash** and **Cash América** brands, offering secured loans (pawn lending), retail merchandise sales, and digital financial services.
