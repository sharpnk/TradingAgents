With a market capitalization of approximately **$30.2 billion**, Circle sits at the intersection of traditional capital markets and blockchain-based digital currency infrastructure. The company operates as a regulated issuer of stablecoins, holding massive reserves of cash and U.S. Treasury securities to back its USDC tokens in circulation.
