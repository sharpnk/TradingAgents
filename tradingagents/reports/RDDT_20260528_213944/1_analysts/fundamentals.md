1. **Massive revenue scaling** – 70%+ YoY growth with operating leverage driving margin expansion
2. **Forward P/E compression** – At 18.99x forward earnings, the stock could re-rate higher as profitability is proven
3. **Net cash fortress** – $2.75B net cash provides acquisition firepower and downside protection

5. **High beta (1.85)** – Significant price volatility
6. **Insider selling** – Post-IPO lockup expirations could pressure stock
