## 1. Company Overview

Canadian Pacific Kansas City Limited is North America's only single-line, transcontinental railway, formed by the landmark merger of Canadian Pacific Railway and Kansas City Southern (completed in 2023). The combined network spans approximately 20,000 miles across Canada, the United States, and Mexico, offering unique cross-border service advantages. CPKC is headquartered in Calgary, Alberta.
